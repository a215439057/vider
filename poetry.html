POETRY IN MOTION

POETRY IN MOTION is a colorful journey containing first-class acrobatics, emotional stories and a world renowned international cast. The show follows episodic moments of friendships at different stages in time and is accompanied by well-known poems and songs – from Heinrich Heine and William Shakespeare to Jan Delay and Johnny Cash. Each scene of this creative format is a self-contained episode. Like in an anthology book, we follow different stories about love, longing and chance encounters, told with dynamic choreographies and stunning acrobatics.
