SHOW CONCEPTS & CREATIVE PRODUCTIONS

Based in Berlin, battleROYAL is an international Show Production company working worldwide on Live Shows, Fairs & Events and Arts & Cultural Projects. We love developing new forms of audience engagement.

HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

  • UNIQUE CONCEPTS

    We create custom-made event and show concepts with highly unique production elements for small to large-scale projects. We deliver breath-taking experiences and emotional moments to engage your audience.

  • SHOW PRODUCTION

    We deliver full-service show productions with many years of industry experience, whether it is casting or choreography, video design or music composition, stage management or show direction.

  • BRAND EXPERIENCE

    Custom-made event concepts for live brand communication are at the forefront of our functionality. Interactive media installations are one of our bold communication tools and we love innovative forms of audience engagement.
