ABOUT US!

Based in Berlin, battleROYAL is an international production company working worldwide on Live Shows, Fairs & Events and specially designed concepts for Arts & Cultural projects.

The company takes pride in delivering exceptional work of beauty and power accompanied by unique twists that separate it from the pack. From concept to production, we offer full-service show productions, including choreography, video and music composition, stage design and production management. Our strength is telling powerful visual stories, as shown at demanding public events such as the Opening Ceremony of the European Capital of Culture Aarhus 2017, BMW Festival Night – The Next 100 Years or the awarded musical production Das Wunder von Bern.
