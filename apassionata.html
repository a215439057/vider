APASSIONATA PRESENTS EQUILA

EQUILA, the new permanent show by APASSIONATA, opened in November 2017 in the multi-million dollar theatre complex ‘Apassionata Park’ in Munich. battleROYAL led the creative team on this unique production. Setting new standards with horses in live entertainment including cutting-edge video tracking and light design, stunning riding stunts, astonishing acrobatics and specially composed music.

EQUILA is directed by battleROYAL’s Brendan Shelper with Meike Arnason as Horse Director and in cooperation with renowned show producer Michael Hildebrandt and awarded composer Martin Lingnau. The audience is taken on an fascinating journey to the land EQUILA. Following the story of the young hero Phero and his loyal horse, together they roam the land eventually becoming legendary champions.

A world-class cast of riders, artists and dancers from over 17 nations thrill the audience at six shows per week in a 1,700 seat theatre with the most modern stage technology.

Press: „A show like the world has never seen.“ (TZ / Germany)
