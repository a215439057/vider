INFINIUM - IMMERSIVE PRODUCTION

Working with a major international brand, DREINULL and three creative giants, battleROYAL has once again developed an immersive experience for 2000 guests – this time in Prague. In collaboration with renowned British food scientist Bompas & Parr, Burning Man heroes Poetic Kinetics and theatre legends Les Enfants Terribles the project lead-time was over 10 months and resulted in an extraordinary live experience. Guests could literally hear, taste, smell and interact their way around the purpose-built mature playground.

Over 50 trained actors whispered hints and stories into the ears of the guests, guiding them around the maze of treats, djs and hidden chambers. In the main room a dynamic ensemble of 16 performers, dancers, flying acrobatics and headline DJs such as Booka Shade and Claptone wow’d guests and kept them dancing until dawn. What an experience.

Thanks to all our partners for this immense collaboration, this was a magic production. #AskUsHow
