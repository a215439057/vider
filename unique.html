B20 SUMMIT

In cooperation with Schrenk + Schrenk, battleROYAL and SLIKK Showacts realized a stunning show performance for the B20 Summit in May 2017, an integral part of the G20 process. Inspired by this year’s motto we did the show direction and video design for futuristic performances which framed the speeches and discussions of leading companies and innovative brands. Our partner label SLIKK delivered several themed showacts like the energetic LED dancers and a dynamic Cyr Wheel performance, which supported the dramaturgy of the evening.

The event took place in the Tempodrom, Berlin, and was attended by some of the world’s finest representatives of economy and politics.

(B20 Summit 2017 an initiave by BDI, DIHK,BDA)
