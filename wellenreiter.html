WELLENREITER

This exklusive production for Mein Schiff 6, the most modern ship of TUI Cruises, was co-developed, choreographed and directed by battleROYAL. “Wellenreiter” tells a humorous and magical adventure story about two scientists and a keen pirate captain who undertake an incredible adventure. They face a monstrous kraken and intelligent algae whilst visiting an unknown land full of exotic magic. Stunning video design, artistic acts and dynamic dance scenes make this show a haunting experience.

Thanks to Chris Moylan, Torsten Hirche and all the TUI crew for the great collaboration.
